About Me

[cite_start]I am an interdisciplinary computational scientist with over eight years of experience in architecting high-performance computing (HPC) solutions[cite: 2]. [cite_start]My core expertise lies in designing scalable, distributed tensor engines for GPUs, which are fundamental to both modern AI/Large Language Models and next-generation quantum simulations[cite: 3].

[cite_start]Currently a postdoctoral researcher at CNRS in Toulouse, I am developing a novel tensor toolchain for an Advanced ERC grant[cite: 9, 10]. [cite_start]My work focuses on the automatic optimization of tensor expressions and enabling distributed memory tensor contractions on GPUs with excellent weak scaling[cite: 10, 11]. [cite_start]I was recently awarded the Marie Curie Fellowship Seal of Excellence with a 91% score for my work on a novel HPC tensor library optimized for Grace-Hopper architecture[cite: 4, 5].
